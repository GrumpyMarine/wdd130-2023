Hi Desert Iris & Daylily Society

Hi-Desert Iris and Daylily Society is a 503(c)(3) non-profit corporation and an educational organization with a goal of promoting Iris and Daylily as a drought-tolerant plant suitable for growing in a desert environment. We are an affiliate of the American Iris Society, Region 15 and founded April 1988.

We live in a desert surrounded by dry to dead plants and lots of dirt. With the education and teamwork of our organization, we would like to show others the beauty of the desert when you add the Irises and Daylilys.
